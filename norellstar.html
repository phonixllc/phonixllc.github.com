Artist Statement

“I saw the angel in the marble and carved until I set him free.” -Michelangelo

"My name is Norell, I am 23 years old and I love to model, sing, act, and perform. I am also an avid writer and I am working on my first novel, which will be released in an application on the Android & Apple Market currently in development. Date of Release: TBA.

Other Interests/Hobbies: I love reading, learning, cooking, making jewelry, painting, photography, as well as meditating and exercising to keep my mind, body and spirit in the best shape possible.

I live and breathe to write, create art and music. I am driven by my inner need to inspire and invoke positive change. I love meeting and collaborating on concepts that are birthed and driven by similar principles with creative artists and musicians. We’re all in this together! Let’s love one another and help a friend, shower a loved one with affection, bless a stranger with a random act of kindness. Radiate loving energy, be mindful of your intentions.

On the Dean’s List of the University of Minnesota – College of Liberal Arts, where I graduated with honors in May of 2011, receiving my Bachelor of Arts Degree in Communications & Proficiency in German.

Singer, Writer, Artist, Advocate for Love & Justice. In the end, Love & Kindness are all we have."

- Norell"

Author

High Seas Heaven Through Hell: Love, Lies & Fighting to Survive

Summary

When a small-town karaoke star with big dreams moves to the city, she meets a modern day wizard and interactive artist while modeling at his warehouse party.

It was the collapse of his life, a standstill for hers—yet the beginning of their life. She stood before him in near nakedness, with music and body painted girls amidst—so begins the story of the modern day artist and his muse, in a sinking economy.

The story continues with Mary in her final year of her collegiate career, and the artist Zander attempting to put his life back together, with harsh realizations for both on fraudulent friends, shallow family, and unforgiving circumstances.

In a short year, they bump heads with red-haired Spricket24, Ellen DeGeneres, media icon Bill Moyers, and butt heads with local medias. They face trials of homelessness in freezing winters and scorching heat, after escaping a Somalian sex trafficking gang, only to fall into harassment of perverted landlords, investigated by the FBI.

They question themselves, each other, and everyone they encounter. This is a story of survival, spiritual journey, and a quest to the inner self. A story so hard to believe, you would think it is fiction. This is a true story.

This will be released in an application, and there will be limited print copies available soon.
