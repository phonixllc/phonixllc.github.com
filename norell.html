Norell sang with Terrell "Carnage" Woods from Rhymesayers entertainment, alongside Elliott Roche, who is now the frontman of the Cheap Thrills. She has also starred in Emily Colay's (Wookiefoot) choir, Cantera. She has done backup vocals for the Artist Brant Kingman's daughter, alongside Emily Colay. She has also sang for MPLS.TV, performed with Elliot Roche (The Cheap Thrills) at Bubble Me, Driftwood Char Bar, and more, as well as performing solo at the Lounge Nightclub in Minneapolis.

She has worked alongside the Artist Zander on his body paint tours including Summer Set Music and Camping Festival, and Sexapalooza in Minneapolis and Portland in 2012, at this year at Benny Benassi, followed by Gay Pride in Minneapolis at the Gay 90's. She also conducted interviews for XTV (founded by Zander) with Amy Klobuchar, Bill Mohers, and Jacob Frey. She has been body painted live in the streets and is currently working on an autobiography, as well as an online gallery featuring her modeling ventures. She is also recording an album and coding an application with Zander.
