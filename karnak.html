Karnak


Karnak Gallery, a venue, art, music, and multimedia collective, was founded in the summer of 2010 by Jacob Alexander Figueroa. The gallery was merely blocks away from the venue made famous by Prince, First Ave.
It housed a tremendous sound system, rotating art installments by both local and international guests, and regularly curated and hosted multi-sensory events on the fourth floor of a historic warehouse building overlooking downtown Minneapolis.
