Born Jacob Alexander Figueroa, he is a technology enthusiast, front & back end developer, and a multimedia artist with artworks featured in NY Arts Magazine, and he is also a renowned body painter. Zander’s body painting was featured at NY Fashion Week 2011, Summer Set Music and Camping Festival 2012, to high caliber shows such as Benny Benassi and Paul Van Dyk, to Black Kat Shows (Portland/Minneapolis), and most recently, MN Gay Pride 2013 and 2014.

Published locally and internationally, Zander is a featured artist in "All Over the Walls", a Blue Bridge Media Group Documentary that premiered at the Minneapolis St Paul International Film Festival in 2011, as well as taking “Best Documentary Film” at the Highway 61 Festival that year, and also featured at the MCAD Underground Film Festival.

Zander’s artworks have been presented alongside Celebrity Designer Emre Erturk and fine artists Gigi Turker, and Gulay Alpay. Jacob Alexander is also proficient in violin, fluent in Spanish and an avid painter, and photographer. He helped develop Audrey Kitching's Crystal Cactus Product Web Page. He is also a contributing member in The Band Famous, adding violin to synthesizers to their soul EDM sound, and unveiled a new body art installment for their debut performance, body painting at the Love Monster Famous App Release Party on Friday the 13th, in June of 2014. He is also a developer, enrolled in Full Sail University and pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Mobile Development. You can download his first live app, The Band Famous, free on the iPhone App Store!
