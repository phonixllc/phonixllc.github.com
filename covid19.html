Driving:

You may know about Uber and Lyft, Postmates and Instacart for ways of making money via your vehicle. Did you know that you can also turn your car into a way to make money via hosting ads? That's right!

With Wrapify.com you can make $264 - $452 per month by installing decal on your car for ads. Cars must be made in the year 2010 or newer, be it a Coupe, Sedan, SUV, pick up truck, or minivan but it must be without significant body/paint damage. You must also pass the following requirements in a background check:

  • • No more than 2 accidents in the past 3 years
  • • No more than 1 moving violation and 1 accident in the past 3 years
  • • No more than 2 moving violations in the past 3 years
  • • No cell phone or distracted driving violations in the past 3 years
  • • No major violations in the past 5 years
  • • Driver must be at least 21 or older

Surveys:

The following are some options which offer some compensation for simple things like taking surveys, and shopping online. Read on to learn about rewards you can earn when you spend money as well.

Swagbucks offers coupons and cash-back when you shop online, and you can also accumulate earnings through their surveys.

SurveyJunkie.com offers points for completing online surveys. Every 362 points you accumulate equate to $5.00 USD.

Lifepointspanel.com offers payments in the form of gift cards or PayPal for completing enough of their surveys. Your views and opinions help influence brands you know and love.

Inboxdollars.com will also pay you money to take surveys, as well as reading emails, playing games, watching videos and more. They'll give you $5.00 just for getting started! You will also have the opportunity to earn money back when you spend money online. You can cash out at the $30 mark.

Prizerebel.com has a point system where every 500 points equal $5.00; 1000 points = $10.00, and so on. They are similar to InboxDollars and Swagbucks by presenting several methods of earning supplemental side income.

With all of these "get paid to take surveys", etc., beware of third-party promotions and we highly advise caution in downloading anything. We also recommend only earning extra money in this fashion if you have a good anti-malware installed, as some third-parties can be spammy. Also, don't overshare and consider using an email for surveys alone (they have a tendency to flood your inbox with survey opportunities).
